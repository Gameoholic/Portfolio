The Startup

An event made for the TopStrix server in collaboration with Ben-Gurion University, to encourage children to innovate and create startups.
Their university was recreated in-game along with a limited time gamemode, which required players to select a startup category, collect resources for it and submit their startup idea for a chance to win.

Hub Elytras

Hub Elytras is a simple plugin that makes your hub more lively by adding elytra spots.
They launch you to the sky and give you an elytra, until you land.
It has cool particle animations created with Partigon.

Fancy 2FA

A 2FA Authentication plugin for Minecraft.
Authenticate yourself via Discord, security questions or a password, all of which are hashed, salted and peppered, and stored in a MySQL database.

Click to read more about it.
