Lumber Defense

The video has sound!

Lumber Defense is a PVE Minigame plugin.
Your objective is to survive attacks from mobs, and to defend your tree.
Some mobs (marked with a sapling in their name) only target the tree, and should be dealt with quickly. Other mobs will attack you and try to slow you down.
The mob types, items, rounds and strings are all configurable.

This project is still under development, with tons of more features to come.
